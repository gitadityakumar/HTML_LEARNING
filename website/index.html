When did Tacos appears in United States?

Tacos made their appearance in the United States in the late 19th century. The exact date is a bit challenging to pinpoint due to the gradual spread of Mexican cuisine across the border, but historical records suggest that tacos began to gain popularity in the southwestern United States during the late 1800s. As Mexican immigrants brought their culinary traditions to the region, tacos became increasingly available, eventually spreading to other parts of the country and becoming a beloved staple of American cuisine.(Source: wikipedia )
